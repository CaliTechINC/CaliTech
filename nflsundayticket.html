What is NFL Sunday Ticket and what games are included?

NFL Sunday Ticket is a premium out-of-market sports package that broadcasts all out-of-market regular season Sunday National Football League games not available on your local broadcast in your area (NBC, CBS or FOX affiliate). NFLST excludes digital only games. For example, if you're a Dallas Cowboys fan but live in Miami, you'd be able to watch all of the Sunday Cowboys games that aren't shown on NBC, CBS or FOX in your local market.
